Agriota E-Marketplace Facilitation Center (AEFC) Programs

CropData's AEFC program is for traders, food processors, exports, stockists, etc, who play the essential role of market makers in the industry and provide the much-needed liquidity through their knowledge and experience. CropData members could trade for themselves or for others and even act as aggregators for others. Being a neutral party, CropData doesn't trade for itself and sees its partners as commodities experts and leverages their expertise to drive the buy-side ecosystem, while providing them end-to-end services for a holistic experience.
